Milestone: Core Game Implementation

Here are some of the potential suggestions and approximate point values:

  • Implement different kinds of weapons (~15 points)
  • Images for the player, enemies, environment, resource items (~8 points)
  • Have power-ups (~10 points)
  • Even more qualitatively different kinds of enemies (~10+ points)
  • Save the game that is in progress, and load previously saved games (~10 points)
  • Maintain a high score list, where you can enter your initials after a successful game (maybe persisted between runs) (~5–10 points)
  • Help screen that explains the keys (minor, but very helpful for graders) (~5 points)
  • Start screen with cool animations (~5–10 points)
  • Animation of sprites for the hero and/or enemies (~10–15 points)
  • Vertical scrolling for tall levels (~20–25 points)

Additional Core-Functionality Requirements

  • Winning a level and advancing to the next one when the hero reaches the safe zone (15 points)
  • The player wins the game after completing the last level and has the option to exit or play again (5 points)

How many points do you expect to earn based on your completion of these final two requirements? (20 points if both are 100% completed)

Milestone: Video Demo

  1. Record 5 min video demoying your game (youtube preferred):
    • The video must be no longer than 5 minutes
    • The video URL **must** be publicly accessible (YouTube link, for example). Panopto video is not accessible outside of the school
    • It must have a clear introduction of all team members and your game objectives (scope and audience)
    • You should walk through at least one level and show all features like shooting, collisions, scores, etc.
    • Specify one extra feature you implemented
    • The demo must be prepared in advance - steps and taking turns, for example. If there is an issue during recording, re-record it
    • Note - when uploading to YouTube, the processing may take long so do NOT upload 5 minutes before the deadline
    • Do not use any copyrighted material (like playing the background music) as YouTube may disqualify or flag your video
    • Make sure the video is published and not private
